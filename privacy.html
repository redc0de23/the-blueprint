Privacy Protocol

Last updated: May 20, 2024

1. Information Collection

THE BLUEPRINT collects minimal personal data. We exclusively collect your email address when you voluntarily initialize enrollment in our protocol. This data is provided directly by you for the purpose of receiving our educational insights.

2. Purpose of Use

The collected email address is used solely to deliver periodic newsletters containing data-driven insights, pattern analysis, and risk management education. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling outside of the delivery of the requested content.

3. Data Integrity & Security

We do not sell, lease, or distribute your email address to third-party commercial entities. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard protocols through our delivery partners (e.g., Kit). We maintain a strictly non-commercial data stance.

4. Sovereignty & Opt-Out

You maintain absolute sovereignty over your data. You may terminate your enrollment at any time by utilizing the "Unsubscribe" link found in the footer of every dispatch. Upon termination, your data is processed for removal from our active protocol list.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may utilize minimal, non-intrusive cookies or anonymous analytics to monitor the technical performance of this site and improve the delivery of our framework. No behavioral tracking for the purpose of advertisement is conducted.

6. Contact

For inquiries regarding this protocol, contact us via the provided support channels within the newsletter dispatches.
